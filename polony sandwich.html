Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients






STEPS

    Step 1 First decide what type of filling you would like and check to see if there is some available.
    Step 2Next take two slices of bread and butter each of them on one side only.

    Step 3 Put your filling on one slice of bread, butter side up.
    Step 4 Place the other piece of bread, butter side down, on top of the filling.
