OREO CAKE

Prep time: 0 mins

Yield: 4

Ingredients






STEPS

    Step 1 Crush Oreo Cookies and make the cake batter. In a blender, crush the Oreo cookies to a fine powder. Now, add milk, sugar, baking powder and blend well. Adjust the quantity of milk in a way it is of pouring consistency.
    Step 2 Microwave the cake for 4-5 minutes. Now,take a microwave-safe bowl and pour the mixture on it. Microwave on high for 4-5 minutes or till a knife inserted in the middle of the cake, comes out clean. Gradually increase the baking time until the cake bakes perfectly.
    Step 3 Decorate your cake and serve Take it out of the dish carefully, place on a platter and pour chocolate ganache, whipped cream or garnish with chopped nuts.
